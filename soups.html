Soups

Soup is one of the world's oldest and most universal foods, said Janet Clarkson, author of the book "Soup: A Global History". "Every culture has soe kind of soup", she said. "It's got very ancient roots". Early people simmered it in everything from turtle shells to lengths of bamboo, she writes in the book, turning out metal soup pots in the Bronze Age.
